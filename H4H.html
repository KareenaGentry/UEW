Our First Project!

Unlocking Eden's first ministry is called Homes for Haiti. It is a for-profit business, locally run and employed. The ministry will be a franchise with the company EcoDom (http://en.ecodom.mx/) established in Mexico. The homes are built out of recycled material with over 2 tons of plastic used per home. This business model provides employment, it is environmentally friendly and focused, and makes something beautiful out of something initially seen as ugly.

Homes for Haiti Mission

"Homes for Haiti follows Christ’s calling to heal the afflicted and care for the poor by turning trash into housing, offering employment which grows self-worth, and transforming whole villages from a few hospitable homes, which allows people to become their full selves by living into who God has called them to be. By this act of love, the people live in a rich relationship with the father and thriving communities are created."

“My people will live in peaceful dwelling places, in secure homes, in undisturbed places of rest.”

More about the Homes!

The homes are secure and earthquake resistant.
It takes just 7 days to build.
Every home includes a living room, 2 bedrooms, a kitchen, and a bathroom.
Each house is made of 2 tons recycled plastic.
